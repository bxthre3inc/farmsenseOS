## Team Culture

### Core Values

1. **Deterministic Thinking** — Decisions based on data, not intuition
2. **Regulatory First** — Build for compliance, not around it
3. **Farmer-Centric** — Every feature must deliver measurable ROI
4. **Scientific Rigor** — Peer-review quality in everything we publish
5. **Strategic Patience** — 10-year infrastructure play, not a quick flip

- **Weekly All-Hands**: Fridays, 30 min — metrics, blockers, wins
- **Monthly Board Updates**: Investor/advisor visibility
- **Quarterly Retreats**: Team alignment, strategic planning
