# FarmSense Farmer Onboarding Funnel

**Version:** 1.0  
**Target:** Convert 1,280 Subdistrict 1 fields to FarmSense subscribers  
**Timeline:** 18-month rollout (June 2026 – Dec 2027)  
**Owner:** FarmSense Growth + Field Operations  

---

## Executive Summary

This document maps the complete farmer journey from first awareness to Enterprise-tier subscription. The funnel is designed for the San Luis Valley's unique context: conservative farmers, water crisis urgency, and regulatory pressure ($500/AF fees).

**Key Metrics:**
- **Target CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost):** $1,200 per field
- **Target LTV (Lifetime Value):** $35,928 (6-year retention at $499/month)
- **LTV:CAC Ratio:** 30:1 (exceptional for ag-tech)
- **Conversion Targets:** Awareness → Trial: 15% | Trial → Basic: 60% | Basic → Enterprise: 25%

## 3. STAGE 2: INTEREST → TRIAL (60% conversion)

### 3.1 The "2-Field Pilot" Demo Program

**What farmers get (free, 30-day):**
- 1 PMT installed on their pivot
- Access to Basic tier dashboard
- Weekly printed report (for farmers without smartphones)
- Field visit from FarmSense technician at day 15

**What FarmSense gets:**
- Real-world sensor validation
- Testimonial video permission
- Conversion to paid subscriber

### 3.2 Trial Installation Workflow

```
Day -7: Farmer signs trial agreement (digital or paper)
Day -3: Site survey — technician visits field, checks pivot type
Day -1: Hardware kit prepared (PMT, mounting bracket, LoRa antenna)
Day 0: Installation (2 hours, farmer present for training)
Day 1: First data flows to dashboard, farmer gets login
Day 7: First weekly report delivered (email + printed if requested)
Day 15: Mid-trial check-in call + optional field visit
Day 21: "Upgrade to Basic" offer sent ($99/mo first 3 months)
Day 30: Trial ends — conversion conversation or extension offer
```

### 3.3 Trial Conversion Tactics

| Tactic | Timing | Owner |
|--------|--------|-------|
| **Water savings alert** | Day 3 (first irrigation optimization) | Automated |
| **Mid-trial check-in** | Day 15 | Field technician |
| **Peer comparison** | Day 20 | "Your field vs. neighbor avg" dashboard feature |
| **Limited-time offer** | Day 25 | "50% off first 3 months if you upgrade now" |
| **Personal call from brodiblanco** | Day 28 | Founder outreach for high-potential farms |

## 4. STAGE 3: TRIAL → BASIC (60% conversion)

### 4.1 Basic Tier Value Proposition

**Price:** $99/month ($1,188/year)

**Includes:**
- 1 PMT per pivot (GNSS tracking, flow monitoring)
- 20-meter resolution soil moisture maps
- Weekly irrigation recommendations (email + SMS)
- Basic dashboard (web + mobile)
- RGWCD compliance export (quarterly)

**Perfect for:** Conservative farmers who want to "test the waters" without full commitment.

### 4.2 Basic Onboarding (First 90 Days)

```
Week 1: "Getting Started" email sequence
  - Dashboard login + tutorial video (5 min)
  - How to read your first irrigation recommendation
  - Support contact info

Week 2: First optimization event
  - "We saved you 2.5 AF this week — $1,250 in fees avoided"
  - Screenshot for farmer to share with neighbors

Week 4: Monthly review call
  - 15-min phone check-in: "How's it working? Any questions?"
  - Record testimonials if positive

Week 8: Enterprise upsell touch
  - "See what you're missing: 1m resolution, predictive maintenance, water court evidence"
  - Offer: "Free LRZ1/LRZ2 scout install if you upgrade this month"

Week 12: Quarterly business review
  - Formal report: Total water saved, ROI achieved, next quarter forecast
  - Hard ask for Enterprise upgrade
```

## 5. STAGE 4: BASIC → ENTERPRISE (25% conversion)

### 5.1 Enterprise Tier Value Proposition

**Price:** $499/month ($5,988/year)

**Includes:**
- Everything in Basic, plus:
- 1-meter resolution (LRZ1/LRZ2 sensor mesh)
- Predictive maintenance (current harmonic analysis)
- Digital Water Ledger (court-admissible blockchain records)
- Water rights trading access (AllianceChain)
- Priority support + annual on-site calibration
- "Resolution Pop" drone audit (2x per season)

**Perfect for:** Large operators, farmers facing water court scrutiny, early adopters.

### 5.2 Enterprise Conversion Triggers

| Trigger | System Response | Upsell Message |
|---------|-----------------|----------------|
| **Flow anomaly detected** | PMT alerts farmer to potential pump issue | "Enterprise predictive maintenance would have caught this 2 weeks ago" |
| **RGWCD audit notice** | Farmer receives compliance investigation letter | "Enterprise Digital Water Ledger provides court-ready evidence" |
| **Water shortage declared** | Subdistrict announces curtailment | "Enterprise tier optimizes every drop — maximize your allocation" |
| **Neighbor trades water rights** | AllianceChain activity visible | "Upgrade to trade your excess water — $5,000+ per season potential" |
| **Basic tier ROI proven** | 6+ months of positive returns | "You've saved $12K — imagine 5x more precision" |

### 5.3 Enterprise Sales Process

```
1. QUALIFY (Discovery call, 30 min)
   - Farm size, crops, current water costs, pain points
   - Decision maker identification (often not the farmer)
   - Timeline and budget confirmation

2. DEMO (Field visit + live dashboard, 2 hours)
   - Show neighbor's Enterprise data (with permission)
   - Live LRZ1/LRZ2 sensor install demonstration
   - ROI projection customized to their field

3. PROPOSAL (Formal written proposal, 48-hour delivery)
   - 3-year contract with annual pricing lock
   - Hardware lease vs. purchase options
   - Service level agreement (SLA)

4. NEGOTIATE (Founder involvement for 50+ acre deals)
   - Custom terms for large operators
   - Multi-field bundle discounts
   - Referral commitment for case studies

5. CLOSE (Contract signature, 2-week implementation)
   - First month free trial of full Enterprise stack
   - "Success metrics" defined upfront
   - Quarterly business review scheduled
```
