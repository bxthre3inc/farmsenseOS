Review site survey map\n2. Verify pivot model and tower spacing\n3. Confirm electrical tap availability (12VDC)\n4. Check weather window (no precipitation forecast 24hr)\n5. Gather tools and PMT kit\n\n**Tools Required:**\n| Tool | Specification | Purpose |\n|------|-------------|---------|\n| Torque wrench | 3/8" drive, 5-25 ft-lb | U-bolt tightening |\n| Multimeter | True RMS, 600V | Voltage verification |\n| GPS unit | Survey-grade, <1m accuracy | Position logging |\n| Tablet | Ruggedized, farm dust rated | Configuration |\n| Safety harness | OSHA compliant | Tower work |\n| Fall protection | 6' lanyard | Tower work |\n\n**Step-by-Step Installation:**\n\n**Step 1: Tower Selection (10 minutes)**\n- Inspect towers 2, 3, and 4 for structural integrity\n- Check for weld cracks, rust, or deformation\n- Select tower with best electrical access\n- Verify clear sky view for GPS (no overhead obstructions)\n- Mark selected tower with spray paint\n\n**Step 2: Bracket Mounting (20 minutes)**\n- Position stainless bracket at desired height (10-15 feet)\n- Align with pivot span direction of travel\n- Install U-bolts with anti-seize compound\n- Torque to 15 ft-lb in star pattern\n- Verify no movement with 50-lb lateral force\n\n**Step 3: Spring Isolator Installation (15 minutes)**\n- Mount 4× Vibrashock VSG-1 isolators to bracket\n- Torque to 8 ft-lb\n- Verify 0.5" travel range\n\n**Step 4: Enclosure Mounting (10 minutes)**\n- Attach Polycase ML-44F to isolators\n- Orient with cable glands facing down\n- Torque screws to 6 ft-lb\n\n**Step 5: Antenna Installation (15 minutes)**\n- Mount LoRa antenna (3ft whip) to top of bracket\n- Spring base oriented for flex in wind\n- Route coax through gland, seal with silicone\n- Mount GPS antenna on 12" mast, clear view of sky\n- Route GPS cable separately from power\n\n**Step 6: Electrical Connection (20 minutes)**\n- Locate 12V tap in pivot panel\n- Verify voltage: 11.5-14.5V acceptable\n- Install 5A inline fuse\n- Route power cable through sealed gland\n- Connect to PMT terminal block (observe polarity)\n- Test: LED indicators illuminate\n\n**Step 7: Flow Meter Installation (30 minutes)**\n- Locate discharge pipe (downstream of pump, upstream of pivot)\n- Clean pipe surface (remove paint, rust)\n- Apply coupling compound\n- Mount TFX-5000 transducers at 45° V-method\n- Separation: 6" for 6" pipe, 12" for 12" pipe\n- Torque clamps to 25 ft-lb\n- Connect cable to PMT\n\n**Step 8: Initial Power-On (10 minutes)**\n- Switch on at pivot panel\n- Observe PMT LED sequence:\n  - Red solid: Booting (30 seconds)\n  - Red blink: GNSS acquisition (up to 10 minutes)\n  - Green solid: RTK fix achieved\n  - Blue blink: LoRa transmission active\n\n**Step 9: GNSS Configuration (20 minutes)**\n- Connect tablet via BLE\n- Launch FarmSense Field Config app\n- Verify RTK base station connectivity\n- Achieve "Fixed" solution (<2cm accuracy)\n- Record position: LAT/LON/EL\n- Save to device registry\n\n**Step 10: IMU Calibration (15 minutes)**\n- Command from app: "Start IMU Calibration"\n- Keep PMT stationary for 30 seconds\n- Verify all 9 axes report valid values\n- Save calibration to flash\n\n**Step 11: LoRa Range Test (15 minutes)**\n- Command: "Test Chirp"\n- Verify DHU acknowledgment\n- Check RSSI: should be >-100dBm\n- If weak, adjust antenna position\n\n**Step 12: Full Rotation Test (30 minutes)**\n- Start pivot in slow mode\n- Monitor GNSS track on app\n- Verify continuous position updates\n- Verify IMU detects motion\n- Stop pivot\n- Verify pivot stop detected\n\n**Step 13: Flow Zero Calibration (15 minutes)**\n- Ensure pump is off\n- Command: "Calibrate Flow Zero"\n- Wait for stable reading (2 minutes)\n- Save zero offset\n\n**Step 14: Documentation (10 minutes)**\n- Photograph installation\n- Record final GPS coordinates (decimal degrees, 8 decimal places)\n- Log firmware version\n- Record battery voltage\n- Sign off on installation certificate\n- Upload to asset management system\n\n**Post-Installation Verification (24-hour):**\n- Check for 24 hours of continuous telemetry\n- Verify hourly chirps received at DHU\n- Confirm battery voltage stable\n- Verify flow readings (when pump operates)\n\n**Total Installation Time:** 4-5 hours per PMT\n**Crew Required:** 2 technicians (one on ground, one on tower)\n\n---\n\n### Additional Crop Library Entries\n\n**Crop: Corn (Maize)**\n\n| Stage | Days | Root Depth | MAD % | Critical kPa |\n|-------|------|------------|-------|--------------|\n|
