Soil core sample to 48" (characterize texture)\n2. Mark installation point (GPS waypoint)\n3. Verify underground utilities clearance\n\n**Installation:**\n1. Auger 6" diameter hole to 50" depth\n2. Insert HDPE SDR9 outer shell\n3. Backfill annulus with native soil (tamped)\n4. Seal top with locking collar\n5. Log GPS coordinates (±30cm accuracy)\n\n**Alpha-Sled Insertion:**\n1. Pre-charge nitrogen to +5 PSI\n2. Verify desiccant pack dry\n3. Lubricate Viton seals\n4. Insert sled with steady pressure\n5. Lock with stainless retaining pin\n6. Verify chirp acknowledgment from PMT\n\n**Post-Installation:**\n1. Pressure test (maintain +5 PSI for 24hr)\n2. Calibrate dielectric against gravimetric sample\n3. Document in asset management system\n4. Schedule first extraction (pre-harvest)\n\n### 8.3.2 PMT Installation SOP\n\n**Tower Selection:**\n- Preferred: Tower 2 or 3 (optimal balance of height and stability)\n- Avoid: Last tower (excessive sway), first tower (motor interference)\n\n**Mounting:**\n1. Attach stainless bracket with U-bolts\n2. Install spring isolators (vibration dampening)\n3. Mount NEMA 4X enclosure\n4. Connect pivot electrical (12V tap)\n5. Install LoRa antenna (3ft whip, spring base)\n6. Install GPS antenna (clear sky view)\n\n**Calibration:**\n1. RTK base station initialization (10 minutes)\n2. Fixed solution confirmation (<2cm accuracy)\n3. Pivot end-stop mapping (full rotation)\n4. Flow meter zero calibration (pump off)\n5. IMU bias calibration (static 30 seconds)\n6. Test chirp to DHU (signal strength >-100dBm)\n\n---\n\n# PART IX: OPERATIONS & EXECUTION\n\n## 9.1 Seasonal Operations Cycle\n\n### 9.1.1 Spring: Post-Planting Insertion (April-May)\n\n**Trigger:** Crops planted, emergence imminent\n**Window:** 2-4 weeks post-planting\n**Duration:** 15 minutes per sensor\n**Crew Size:** 2 technicians per field\n\n**Workflow:**\n1. Extract Alpha-Sleds from Sled Hospital (winter storage)\n2. Diagnose batteries (voltage check)\n3. Nitrogen recharge to +5 PSI\n4. Insert into permanent HDPE shells\n5. Verify chirp acknowledgment\n6. Log deployment in system\n\n### 9.1.2 Summer: Active Monitoring (June-August)\n\n**Monitoring:**\n- Telemetry: Continuous (1-hour chirp default)\n- Anomaly response: 15-minute "Focus Ripple" mode\n- Irrigation events: Real-time tracking\n\n**Maintenance:**\n- Weekly: DHU solar panel cleaning\n- Bi-weekly: Pressure-decay tests (VFA nitrogen)\n- Monthly: PMT battery voltage checks\n\n### 9.1.3 Fall: Pre-Harvest Extraction (September-October)\n\n**Trigger:** 2 weeks before projected harvest\n**Duration:** 10 minutes per sensor\n**Storage:** Sled Hospital facility (RSS)\n\n**Workflow:**\n1. Unlock retaining pins\n2. Extract Alpha-Sleds with stainless cable\n3. Cap HDPE shells with blanking plugs\n4. Transport to Sled Hospital\n5. Deep-diagnostics (full sensor validation)\n6. Trickle-charge batteries\n7. Store at 15°C, 40% RH\n\n### 9.1.4 Winter: Hibernation (November-March)\n\n**Sled Hospital Operations:**\n- Temperature: Maintained 15-20°C\n- Battery: Trickle charge (0.1C rate)\n- Diagnostics: Monthly health checks\n- Firmware: Quarterly updates (if needed)\n- Calibration: Annual recertification\n\n**Permanent Shells:**\n- Blanking plugs prevent water ingress\n- No maintenance required\n- Visual inspection only (annual)\n\n---\n\n## 9.2 Maintenance Operations\n\n### 9.2.1 Sled Hospital\n\n**Facility:** Modified 20' container at RSS\n**Capacity:** 500 Alpha-Sleds (phased inventory)\n\n**Workflow:**\n| Step | Action | Duration |\n|------|--------|----------|\n| 1 | Receiving (from field) | 5 min |\n| 2 | External cleaning | 10 min |\n| 3 | Deep-diagnostic (full test) | 30 min |\n| 4 | Battery analysis/replacement | 15 min |\n| 5 | Nitrogen recharge | 5 min |\n| 6 | Firmware update (if needed) | 10 min |\n| 7 | Storage assignment | 5 min |\n| **Total** | | **80 min per sled** |\n\n### 9.2.2 Field Service Protocol\n\n**Response Times:**\n| Severity | Issue | Response | Resolution |\n|----------|-------|----------|------------|\n| 1 | PMT failure | 4 hours | Replacement |\n| 2 | VFA/LRZ offline | 24 hours | Diagnosis |\n| 3 | PFA fault | 2 hours | Emergency stop verification |\n| 4 | DHU offline | 8 hours | Field visit |\n\n---\n\n## 9.3 Fleet Operations Scale\n\n**Subdistrict 1 (1,280 fields):**\n| Metric | Value |\n|--------|-------|\n| Total nodes | 25,600 |\n| Sled Hospital throughput | 80 sleds/day |\n| Extraction window | 30 days |\n| Technicians required | 6 FTE |\n| Vehicles | 3 (service trucks) |\n\n**Annual Cycles:**\n- Insertion: 25,600 sleds (April)\n- Extraction: 25,600 sleds (September)\n- Hospital processing: 51,200 operations/year\n
