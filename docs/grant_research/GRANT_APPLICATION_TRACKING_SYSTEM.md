Opportunities**\n\n## System Overview\n\nThis document defines the tracking infrastructure for Bxthre3/FarmSense's global grant pipeline. \n\n## Tracking Schema\n\n### Grant Record Structure\n\n```\nGRANT_ID: [CATEGORY]-[REGION]-[NUMBER]\n  Example: FED-US-001, FDN-GLB-015, STA-CO-003\n\nFIELDS:\n├── Basic Information\n│   ├── grant_name (string)\n│   ├── funder (string)\n│   ├── category (enum: FEDERAL, STATE, FOUNDATION, INTERNATIONAL, CORPORATE)\n│   ├── region (string: US, CO, CA, EU, AF, AS, SA, OC)\n│   └── url (string)\n│\n├── Financial\n│   ├── max_amount (number)\n│   ├── min_amount (number)\n│   ├── currency (enum: USD, EUR, GBP, AUD, CAD)\n│   └── funding_type (enum: GRANT, PRIZE, EQUITY, DEBT, HYBRID)\n│\n├── Timeline\n│   ├── deadline (date)\n│   ├── deadline_type (enum: HARD, ROLLING, INVITE)\n│   ├── award_date (date)\n│   └── project_duration (months)\n│\n├── Status Tracking\n│   ├── stage (enum: IDENTIFIED, RESEARCHING, PITCHING, APPLIED, SHORTLIST, AWARDED, REJECTED, WITHDRAWN)\n│   ├── priority (enum: P0, P1, P2, P3)\n│   ├── probability (percent)\n│   └── expected_value (calculated: amount × probability)\n│\n├── Application Details\n│   ├── application_portal (string)\n│   ├── account_created (boolean)\n│   ├── requirements_checklist (array)\n│   ├── materials_submitted (array)\n│   └── notes (text)\n│\n└── Team Assignment\n    ├── lead_applicant (string)\n    ├── support_team (array)\n    └── external_partners (array)\n```\n\n## Stage Definitions\n\n| Stage | Definition | Action Required |\n|-------|-----------|-----------------|\n| **IDENTIFIED** | Grant discovered, basic info captured | Research eligibility |\n| **RESEARCHING** | Eligibility confirmed, requirements known | Gather materials, assess fit |\n| **PITCHING** | Initial outreach/LOI submitted | Prepare full application |\n| **APPLIED** | Full application submitted | Track status, prepare for interview |\n| **SHORTLIST** | Advanced to final round | Prepare presentation, negotiate |\n| **AWARDED** | Funding secured | Execute contract, begin project |\n| **REJECTED** | Declined | Capture feedback, archive |\n| **WITHDRAWN** | Voluntarily withdrawn | Document reason |\n\n## Priority Matrix\n\n```\nPRIORITY = f(Amount, Deadline, Probability, Strategic Fit)\n\nP0 (CRITICAL): >$500K, <30 days, >25% probability, core mission\nP1 (HIGH): >$250K, <90 days, >15% probability, strong alignment  \nP2 (MEDIUM): >$100K, <180 days, >10% probability, good fit\nP3 (LOW): <$100K, >180 days, any probability, opportunistic\n```\n\n## Automation Rules\n\n### Date-Based Triggers\n- **T-30 days**: Move P2 → P1 if materials ready\n- **T-14 days**: Daily standup on P0 grants\n- **T-7 days**: Final review required\n- **T-3 days**: Submit or withdraw decision\n- **T+1 day**: Archive if not submitted\n\n### Status-Based Actions\n- **IDENTIFIED → RESEARCHING**: Auto-assign to grant queue\n- **RESEARCHING → PITCHING**: Create application workspace\n- **APPLIED → SHORTLIST**: Schedule prep call\n- **REJECTED**: Capture funder feedback, update probability model\n\n## Dashboard Views\n\n### 1.
